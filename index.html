About Us

Motofarm is a purpose built & an exciting dirt-filled racing playground, offering guests a mix of motocross, enduro & flat tracking. Conceptualised to deliver a technically-challenging race circuit imaginable.

The track is super fast, wide, long sweeping and also features tight, twisty turns, banked corners, huge jumps thrown in good measure purely designed to encourage plenty of close, hard-charging race action all the way to the chequered flag!. And is also awesome for banging bars with your buddies, honing your mx skills or just plain old twist-the-throttle fun!!  

Don't be surprised to see rider’s 40ft in the air, upside-down and holding on with just one hand!

WE CARE ABOUT YOU

Security

At MOTOFARM, nothing is more important to us than the safety of our customers, which is why we have gone to such pains to set up a secure and safe environment suitable for beginners and experts alike. Of course, nobody can prevent every accident: this is, after all, a motor sport. However, we can ensure that you are in the safest of hands in the event of the occasional fall or slip on the circuit. All our track marshals are hand-picked for their diligence with emphasis on motor sport injuries, they are fully trained in emergency resuscitation and first aid. Their experience is supplemented by an on-site paramedic and medics with full training.

Facility

The Practice Facility caters to riders of all levels by offering a motocross track that is both safe and challenging to beginning and professional riders, The width and diversity of this track allows riders of all ages and skill to enjoy the track at their own pace . With its constantly changing terrain level, the Practice Facility track is natural, smooth, and  demanding due to the  multitude of obstacles and the soil’s varying transition from loam, to clay, to loose soil. Additionally, riders will have to navigate a series of obstacles that include table top jumps, a big rhythm section, a single & double jump and a big finishing jump.

The centre includes washing stations for cleaning your bike and shower facilities for cleaning yourself! We also offer an on-site cafeteria when you’re in need of a pit stop, as well as a burger van if you fancy a quick snack on the move. We are obsessively passionate about it.
